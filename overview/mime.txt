MIME 

   Q: What does MIME stand for?

   Multipurpose Internet Mail Extension


   Q: Other names for it?

  - MIME type, media type,   
     content type


   Q: What is it?

  - a two-part identifier composed of a type, a subtype, and optional parameters.


   Q: What is it used for?

   - for file formats and format contents transmitted over the Internet like text/html; charset=UTF-8.
  -  for denoting email message contents and attachments.
  - Also used with HTTP & HTML.


   Q: EXAMPLES?

  - application/javascript
  - application/json
  - application/pdf
  - audio/mpeg3   (.mp3)
  - text/css
  - text/html
  - text/plain
  - image/gif
  - image/jpeg
  - image/png
  - video/avi
  - video/mpeg
  - video/quicktime  (.mov)
